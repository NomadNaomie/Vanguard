About us

Originally operating on Kijiji ads, we would buy broken phones, fixing them and flipping them as well as placing repair ads for near cost to build word of mouth. Quickly a workshop was established in Janice's garage and employee #2, Aza, was hired to help fix iPhones and iPods, a far more lucrative venture.

Eventually outgrowing the garage, but not the can-do attitude and need to rely on Kijiji ads, a portion of a warehouse was rented and employee #3, Sam was hired on to be a guiding voice for the company. It's at that time that the name Vanguard was chosen and plans to expand beyond marketplace ads were formed.

Finally a store front was purchased and our employee #4 was hired, Jeff. With positive word of mouth and good location we have been able to consistently set new records for phones repaired in a month and are actively hiring a 5th employee!
